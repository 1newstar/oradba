<H1>Overview</H1>

A series of example scripts for working with DBMS_SCHEDULE. Scripts are provided for enabling, disabling at a job level, purging a job log and amending any attribute of a job.

For creating a new schedule/job see the statspack example and clone/adjust to your preference.
